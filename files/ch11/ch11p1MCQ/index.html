Problem

A mass on a spring in SHM (Fig. 11â€“1) has amplitude A and period T. At what point in the motion is the velocity zero and the acceleration zero simultaneously?

(a) x = A

(b) x > 0 but x < A.

(c) x =0.

(d) x < 0.

(e) None of the above.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    In simple harmonic motion, the velocity is zero at turning pointsand acceleration is maximum. Acceleration is zero at equilibrium positionand velocity is maximum. At the remaining all positions, the velocity and acceleration are nonzero. This means that there are no positions or points where the acceleration and velocity are simultaneously zero.

    Therefore, the option (e) is correct answer.
