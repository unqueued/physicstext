Problem

How did geophysicists determine that part of the Earth’s interior is liquid?

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    Longitudinal waves can propagate through solid, liquid and gases medium. But transverse waves can propagate only through solids. Transverse waves could not pass through any fluid medium because there is no restoring force for liquid.

    When earth quake occurs from the epicenter longitudinal waves and transverse are released. Much variation in the speed of longitudinal waves is observed by geophysicists after earthquake. So, they have concluded that one part of Earth’s interior is in liquid state.
