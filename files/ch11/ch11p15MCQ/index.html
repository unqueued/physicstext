Problem

A wave transports

(a) energy but not matter.

(b) matter but not energy.

(c) both energy and matter

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    A wave is in general disturbance from equilibrium position which is being propagated from one region of space into another region of space. This movement of disturbance is called wave propagation. In wave motion the disturbance (energy) is being propagated through the continuous vibration of the particles of the medium. But the particles of the medium will not leave their original positions. So, in wave propagation, the energy transports but not matter.
